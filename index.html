Homework 1 CS 360: Data Visualization

Jaden Hamid

These are the data visualizations that I did in Tableau then implemented into D3.js

Data

My data was provided by Data SF

San Francisco International Airport data on Landings Statistics (DATASF).
I used a three column data set for the stacked bar
I used 2 a data column set for the pie chart

    Stacked Bar Chart
  1. Month This column gives me the month that the flight occured
  2. Domestic This column give me the sum of all the passenger counts on Domestic flights
  3. International This column give me the sum of all the passenger counts on International flights
    Pie Chart
  1. Boarding Area This column gives me the boarding area that the flight occured
  2. Passenger Count This column give me the sum of all the passenger counts per boarding area over 2017-2019

Wrangling

For the bar chart, I really only cared about the passenger count, the month the flight occured, and whether it was domestic or international.

For the pie chart, I really only cared about the passenger count, the boarding area
